Patratu Dam
Take a ride

Patratu dam is a beautiful tourist spot and people also visit here for picnic. Adjacent to the dam, there is an ancient temple named Panchawahini known for its significant religious value.

The Patratu dam was constructed with the aim to supply water to Patratu Thermal Power Station but the twisting way of architecture on hills and beautiful sceneries made it great tourist attraction. This dam holds storage capacity of 81 square miles. Water from the river Nalkarni and from the waterfalls of the hills surrounding this dam is stored in this dam.
